The Science Behind Metabolic Health

Evidence-based approaches to understanding and optimizing your metabolism

THE SCIENCE

Backed by Rigorous Research

Our approach is grounded in the pioneering work of Dr. Joseph Kraft and decades of metabolic research. We've developed proprietary algorithms that identify early markers of metabolic dysfunction often missed by conventional testing.

By focusing on hyperinsulinemia - the earliest detectable abnormality in metabolic health - we can intervene years before traditional diabetes markers appear. Our testing methodology captures both fasting and post-prandial responses to provide a complete picture of your metabolic health.
