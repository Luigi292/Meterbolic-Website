Cookies Policy

Last Updated: [Date]

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Track effectiveness of our marketing

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Specifically, we use:

  • Session cookies (expire when you close your browser)
  • Persistent cookies (remain until deleted or expired)
  • First-party cookies (set by our website)
  • Third-party cookies (set by our service providers)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Set notifications when cookies are set

Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use services that may set cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel for marketing effectiveness
  • Stripe for payment processing

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our use of cookies evolves. We will notify you of significant changes.

7. Contact Us

For questions about our use of cookies, contact us at privacy@meterbolic.com.
